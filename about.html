About us

A moody beginning

It all began one rainy afternoon in a coffee shop. My husband had planned to meet me there. I had just sat with a coffee and a cake pop. My husband walked in, dropped divorce papers on the table and left. Stunned, I glanced at my pretty decorated cake pop. I got angry at it's quaint, colourful icing. I grabbed it and ate it right then and there. Moody Pops was born.

Moody Pops fills the need for empathy in baking. You may not be able to find a reflection of how you feel in others. But here at Moody Pops, we get you!
